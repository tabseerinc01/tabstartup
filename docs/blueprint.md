# **App Name**: TabEdge

## Core Features:

- User Authentication and Account Management: Allow users to sign up, log in, and manage personal and/or business accounts.
- Invoice Creation: Enable users to create and customize invoices with details, line items, and branding options.
- Payment Link Generation: Allow users to generate unique payment links associated with their personal or business accounts.
- Payment Collection: Enable users to collect payments through generated invoices and payment links.
- Withdrawal Functionality: Allow users to withdraw collected funds to their linked bank accounts.
- Transaction History: Maintain a detailed transaction history, including status (paid, unpaid), type (debit, credit), and transaction details.
- Branded Treasury Service Tool: Using the Stripe Treasury API, provide users with bank accounts and card issuing features, ensuring that the integration remains invisible to the end-user to ensure your branding is consistent throughout the platform. The AI tool will consider factors such as risk and account history when providing insights and suggestions regarding Treasury features.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#7ad2f7), providing a professional and trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Light pale blue (#eaf7fc). The background should be unobtrusive in order to give emphasis to content. It is similar in hue to the primary, and very desaturated.
- Accent color: Indigo (#2b0b9b) to complement the primary color, while helping important elements to stand out.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, giving a modern and objective look.
- Use professional and simple icons for navigation, account settings, and transaction types.
- Maintain a clean, intuitive, and professional layout, focusing on ease of use and clear information hierarchy.
- Incorporate subtle animations for loading states and transitions to enhance user experience.
